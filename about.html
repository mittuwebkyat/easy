About EasyStamp

Pre-inked rubber stamps are a convenient and mess-free option for stamping documents and other materials. They are made of a durable rubber die that is attached to a plastic base. The die is impregnated with ink, so there is no need for an ink pad. To use a pre-inked stamp, simply press it down onto the surface you want to stamp. The ink will transfer to the surface, leaving a clear and sharp impression.

Benefits of using pre-inked rubber stamps:

  • Convenience: Pre-inked stamps are ready to use right out of the package. There is no need to mess with an ink pad.
  • Cleanliness: Pre-inked stamps are mess-free. There is no risk of spilling ink.
  • Durability: Pre-inked stamps are made of durable materials that can withstand repeated use.
  • Sharpness: Pre-inked stamps produce clear and sharp impressions.
  • Versatility: Pre-inked stamps can be used on a variety of surfaces, including paper, cardboard, and fabric.

Tips for using pre-inked rubber stamps:

  • Make sure the surface you are stamping on is clean and dry.
  • Press the stamp down firmly and evenly.
  • Hold the stamp in place for a few seconds to allow the ink to transfer to the surface.
  • If you are stamping on a porous surface, such as paper, you may want to blot the ink with a paper towel to prevent it from smudging.

Uses for pre-inked rubber stamps:

  • Stamping addresses on envelopes
  • Stamping signatures on documents
  • Stamping dates on paperwork
  • Stamping logos on products
  • Stamping artwork and designs
